    Music Composition/Performance

    Music has been a big part of my life for as long as I can remember. I'm mainly a classical percussionist, but I've also dabbled in piano, flute, saxophone, French Horn, guitar, gamelan, and erhu.

    I've performed in orchestras, concert bands, marching bands, choirs, percussion ensembles, gamelan ensembles, and instrumental bands.

    Some of my weirder instruments: flower pots, baking pans.

    I love music so much, I started writing my own pieces.

    As a member and officer of UC Berkeley's Undergraduate Composers Club, I composed and performed several original compositions. In the clips below, I am performing on marimba.
